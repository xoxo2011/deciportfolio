Three Emotion Symbols Challenge

Energy :

why? Triangles are linked to energy, tension, and fear. Adding sharp, zigzag lines can further emphasize intensity and movement.

Calm :

why? Circles promote a feeling of calm. You can enhance this by using soft, curved lines to suggest peaceful ripples or gentle movement.

Confidence :

why? Squares represent stability and confidence. Building with stacked squares can reinforce the idea of a solid, dependable structure.
